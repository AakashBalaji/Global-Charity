Nepal Earthquake


On April 25, 2015 a 7.8 magnitude earthquake hit Nepal. In case you don't know where Nepal is, it is a landlocked country in the Himalayas situated next to India, Bangladesh and China. This earthquake so far has killed and injured over thousands of individuals. Tens of thousands of children and individuals have also been displaced by this disastrous event. Experts estimate that in order to rebuild Nepal, it will take over 10 billion US dollars.
